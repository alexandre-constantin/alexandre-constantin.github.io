My name is Alexandre Constantin, I received the Engineering Degree from ENSEEIHT in Electronics and Signal Processing, Toulouse INP, France, and the M.Sc. Degree from National Taiwan University, Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering (NTU-GICE) both in 2018.

I am now a PhD student in Applied Mathematics from Laboratoire Jean-Kuntzmann in STATIFY team, hosted by Inria.
Entitled Time-series Analysis of Massive Satellite Images: an Ecosystems Mapping Implementation, this PhD is co-funded by Grenoble Institute of Technology and CNES (the French government space agency).
My advisors are Stephane Girard and Mathieu Fauvel.
